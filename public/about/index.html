Who is this guy?

My name is Jordan, otherwise known as thedevbones. I am 22 years old and recently graduated from Texas State University with a Bachelors in Computer Science. My goal is to make fun games, hopefully one day for a living.

What do you do?

Since 2023, I've been teaching myself Godot through several personal projects, including my first solo release, Rudimentary. In late 2025, I joined Deer Shrimp Studios. Together we made H.E.M.A. for Brackeys' 2026 Jam and are currently working on bringing it to a full Steam release. I've also prototyped in Unreal and Unity, and use Blender and Photoshop to create programmer art that fits my retro, low-poly aesthetic.
