Who the heck is this guy? My name is Jordan, otherwise known as thedevbones. I am 21 years old and a university student studying computer science. My goal is to make fun games, hopefully one day for a living. I owe a huge thanks to games like LittleBigPlanet and Dark Cloud that inspired me in my early life.


What does this guy do? Over the past 10 years I've created many small game projects in Game Maker 6.1 and Game Maker Studio which I hope never see the light of day. I have also been using Blender as a way to create "programmer art" that meets my retro, low-poly aesthetic.
